Websites for shops, makers and small retail businesses.

PBI’s retail template lets small shops show products, explain the brand, take enquiries and prepare simple online checkout through Stripe.

Start with a product-led website

Use the retail template to show collections, featured products, shop information, contact details and brand story.

Add up to 10 products

Products can include name, description, price, stock quantity, SKU and image. This is ideal for small ranges, gift boxes, vouchers, hampers, merch or limited collections.

Use Stripe for checkout

Retail checkout uses Stripe, with a connected account flow so the business can receive payments directly after Stripe onboarding is complete.

Keep it simple before going bigger

This is a practical retail starter system, not a full warehouse-management kraken. It gives small businesses a way to sell without needing a full ecommerce platform immediately.

Yes, once Stripe is connected and checkout has been tested.

Does stock reduce after payment?

Yes, paid Stripe webhooks can mark orders paid and reduce stock.

Is this a full Shopify replacement?

Not yet. It is a focused retail starter for small product ranges.
