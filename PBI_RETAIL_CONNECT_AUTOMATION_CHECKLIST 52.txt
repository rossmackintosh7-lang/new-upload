PBI RETAIL CONNECT AUTOMATION UPGRADE

What this adds:
- Customer clicks Connect Stripe inside the Retail shop panel.
- PBI creates/loads a Stripe Express connected account automatically.
- PBI creates a Stripe-hosted onboarding link automatically.
- Customer completes Stripe onboarding with Stripe.
- PBI stores the connected acct_... ID automatically.
- PBI writes the connected account into the current project automatically.
- Retail checkout uses that connected account automatically.
- No manual acct_... copy/paste is needed.

What cannot be automated:
- Stripe identity/business verification. The customer must complete Stripe's hosted onboarding/KYC form.
- PBI platform Stripe setup. You still need STRIPE_SECRET_KEY and STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET set as Cloudflare Secrets.
- Stripe Connect must be available on the PBI Stripe account.

After upload:
npx wrangler d1 execute d1-template-database --file=./database/pbi-combined-schema.sql --remote

Test flow:
1. Log in as a retail customer.
2. Open a shop/retail project in /builder/.
3. Enable Retail shop.
4. Click Connect Stripe.
5. Complete Stripe-hosted onboarding.
6. Return to PBI builder.
7. Check the status says connected/ready.
8. Add products and stock.
9. Save, publish, and test basket checkout.
