Built around real small business needs

A small business website has to do practical work: explain what you offer, build trust, show examples, take enquiries, sell products where needed and make it easy for people to contact you. PBI is designed around that journey rather than dropping you into a blank page and hoping for the best.

Start with templates that already make sense

Choose from example websites for cafés, tradespeople, salons, shops, consultants and holiday lets. Each example includes wording, layout, images and buttons so you can start from a working structure rather than a flat empty canvas.

Use AI help without losing control

PBI can help shape headings, page wording and improvement ideas, while you stay in control of colours, images, pages, layout, calls-to-action and publishing choices.

Get support when DIY is not enough

Some people want to build everything themselves. Others want help with wording, images, domains, Stripe, SEO or launch checks. PBI gives you both routes: self-build tools and optional support.

FAQs

Common questions

Is PBI suitable for very small businesses?

Yes. PBI is aimed at small businesses that need a clean, practical website without starting from a blank screen.

Can I publish without a custom domain?

Yes. You can start with a PBI subdomain, then connect or register a custom domain later.

Can PBI help after I have built the site?

Yes. Assisted Setup, custom builds and SEO Care are available if you want help polishing or improving the site.
