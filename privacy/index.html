Privacy Notice

Plain-English working version for public launch. Review this with a qualified adviser before scaling or adding wider tracking/automation.

What we collect

When you use PBI we may collect your name, email address, website/project details, enquiry messages, billing references, domain choices and support requests.

Why we use it

We use this information to create accounts, save website projects, publish websites, respond to enquiries, provide support, process payments and improve the service.

Who we share it with

We may use trusted providers such as Cloudflare for hosting/security, Stripe for payments, Resend for emails and OpenAI-powered tools for optional AI assistance.

How long we keep it

We keep account and project data while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterwards for support, legal, accounting and security purposes.

Your choices

You can contact PBI to ask about the personal data held about you, request corrections, ask for deletion where appropriate, or ask questions about how your data is used.
