Why PBI exists

Getting a website built should not feel harder than running the business.

Agencies can be expensive, DIY builders can feel clunky, and many AI websites still look like they came out of a plastic mould.

PBI was created to make the process easier. The aim is simple: help businesses create websites that look professional, explain what they do clearly, and feel like they actually belong to them.

With AI-assisted setup, editable templates, business-focused wording and simple design choices, Purbeck Business Innovations gives local businesses a faster and more affordable route to getting online.
