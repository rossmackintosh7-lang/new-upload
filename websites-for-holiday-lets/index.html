PBI SEO guide • holiday let website

Websites for holiday lets, glamping and guest accommodation.

Accommodation websites need strong images, clear facilities, local area detail and an easy booking or enquiry route.

View holiday let example View templates

Sell the stay, not just the building

Use images and wording that show what guests experience: location, views, comfort, facilities, nearby walks, food, beaches or attractions.

Make practical details clear

Guests need to know sleeping arrangements, amenities, parking, accessibility notes, rules, check-in information and how to book.

Link to booking or enquiry routes

Buttons can go to your booking engine, contact form, Airbnb listing or direct enquiry route.

Create useful local content

Pages about the local area, things to do and nearby places can help guests and improve the usefulness of the website.
