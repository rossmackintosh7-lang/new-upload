What the form does:
- Collects name, business name, email, phone, enquiry type, subject, project name and message.
- Validates the public Turnstile check if TURNSTILE_SECRET_KEY is set.
- Saves the enquiry into support_requests in D1.
- Sends an email to CUSTOM_BUILD_NOTIFY_TO / SUPPORT_NOTIFY_TO / first PBI_ADMIN_EMAILS address using Resend.
- Sends an optional confirmation email to the customer unless PBI_SEND_ENQUIRY_CONFIRMATION is set to "false".
- Shows a clear browser success/error message.
- Does not rely on mailto links or the customer's default email app.

Important Cloudflare requirements:
- DB binding must point to d1-template-database.
- RESEND_API_KEY must be set as a Secret for email sending.
- CUSTOM_BUILD_NOTIFY_TO should point to your receiving address.
- CUSTOM_BUILD_NOTIFY_FROM should be a verified sender, e.g. PBI <enquiry@purbeckbusinessinnovations.co.uk>.
- TURNSTILE_SECRET_KEY should be set as a Secret in Production and Preview.

Admin:
- Form entries appear in the Admin Panel under the Support tab.
