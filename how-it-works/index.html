A guided route from idea to live website

PBI keeps the website process simple: answer a few questions, build your first version, choose a domain and publish when ready.

What makes it different?

PBI is designed for business owners who want a practical website journey rather than a blank page and a thousand confusing settings.

01

Create your account

Sign up, accept the terms and open your dashboard.

02

Choose your business type

Select the style and pages that best match your business.

03

Add your content

Add your business name, wording, services, images, logo and contact details.

04

Preview on mobile and desktop

Check the website layout before publishing.

05

Choose a domain

Use a PBI subdomain, connect your own domain or search for a new domain.

06

Publish and improve

Choose a plan, publish the site and add support or SEO Care if needed.
