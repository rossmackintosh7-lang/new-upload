How it works

Choose a working demo, then turn it into the customer’s website

02

Open in the builder

The chosen demo preloads the builder with colours, structure, images and page text so there is something real to work from immediately.

03

Personalise it

Change the wording, upload the client’s logo and images, adjust pages, pick the domain and fine-tune the design.

04

Publish and support

When the site is ready, move to payment, connect the domain and publish. You can then add assisted setup, a custom build or SEO Care if needed.

Customers see something complete instead of an empty builder. That makes the whole journey easier to understand and far more confidence-building.
