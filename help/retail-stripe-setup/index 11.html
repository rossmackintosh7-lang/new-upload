PBI retail guide

Connect Stripe before taking product payments.

Retail templates let customers add products, stock and a basket, but Stripe must be connected before live checkout can take payments.

What the customer does

  1. Open the PBI project in the builder.
  2. Go to Retail shop.
  3. Click Connect Stripe.
  4. Stripe opens a secure hosted onboarding form.
  5. Create or log in to Stripe and complete the requested business verification.
  6. Return to PBI. PBI stores the connected account automatically.
  7. Add up to 10 products with names, descriptions, prices, stock and images.
  8. Set the shipping label and amount.
  9. Choose whether Stripe Tax should be switched on.
  10. Save, publish and run a small test order before selling publicly.

What PBI handles automatically

  • Sending the customer to Stripe’s secure onboarding flow.
  • Storing the connected account once Stripe returns the customer to PBI.
  • Using the connected account for retail checkout.
  • Recording orders in the retail order dashboard.
  • Reducing stock after a paid Stripe webhook is received.

Important payment note

The customer must complete Stripe’s own verification. PBI cannot skip identity, business or bank checks, because Stripe needs those details before allowing real payments and payouts.

Before going live

  • Check product prices and stock.
  • Check shipping cost.
  • Check the checkout button works.
  • Confirm the order appears in the retail orders area.
  • Confirm stock reduces after payment.
