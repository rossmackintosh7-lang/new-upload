PBI launch guide

Publish when the important parts are ready.

Publishing should feel controlled. Check the basics, choose your domain route, confirm your plan and test the live site.

Before you publish

Use this check before sending your site into the wild with a tiny ceremonial trumpet.

  • Homepage clearly says what the business does.
  • Contact details are correct.
  • Buttons go to the right places.
  • Images load properly on desktop and mobile.
  • Service/product wording is clear.
  • Domain choice is correct.
  • Any retail products, prices and stock are checked.
  • Legal, privacy or policy pages are added where needed.

After publishing

Open the live website in a fresh browser tab and click through every main button. Check mobile view too. Search engines may take time to notice the site, so the first job is making sure customers can use it cleanly.
