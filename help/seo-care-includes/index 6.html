PBI SEO Care

Keep your website improving after launch.

SEO Care is PBI’s monthly improvement support for keeping your website clear, useful, search-friendly and up to date.

What SEO Care includes

SEO Care is not a magic ranking button. It is a practical monthly support service designed to keep your website moving in the right direction.

Each month, PBI can review your website, check for common SEO issues, improve page wording, strengthen calls-to-action, tidy structure, review image text, and suggest useful new content based on what your business offers.

Monthly checks can include

  • Reviewing page titles and meta descriptions.
  • Checking key pages for clear wording and useful structure.
  • Improving headings, calls-to-action and customer journey flow.
  • Checking images, alt text and mobile layout basics.
  • Spotting missing pages, weak content or unclear service wording.
  • Suggesting new pages, FAQs or local search content ideas.
  • Reviewing internal links so visitors can move through the site easily.
  • Helping keep the website fresh, relevant and easier for customers to understand.

What SEO Care does not promise

Search rankings can never be guaranteed. Google, competitors, local demand and search behaviour all change. What PBI can do is help make the website clearer, healthier and more useful over time.

Why it matters

A well-maintained website gives customers and search engines clearer signals about who you are, what you offer and why people should contact you.
