PBI SEO guide • websites for tradespeople

Websites for tradespeople that build trust quickly.

A trades website needs to show what you do, where you work, why customers can trust you and how to request a quote without hunting for contact details.

View tradesperson example View templates

Show your services clearly

Use dedicated sections for plumbing, heating, electrical, building, landscaping, repairs or specialist work so customers can quickly see whether you cover the job.

Build trust before the first call

Add work photos, service areas, qualifications, reviews, guarantees, insurance notes and response times where relevant.

Make enquiries easy

Quote buttons, phone links, enquiry forms and clear service area wording help turn a visitor into a lead.

Use local wording naturally

Mention the towns and areas you actually serve. Local relevance should be helpful, not stuffed into every sentence like gravel in a sandwich.
