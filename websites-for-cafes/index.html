Websites for cafés, restaurants and food businesses.

Food businesses need websites that show atmosphere, menus, opening hours, location and the next step, whether that is booking, ordering, enquiring or visiting.

View café example View templates

Make the food and atmosphere visible

Use strong images for food, coffee, counters, seating and the feel of the place. Customers decide quickly when food is involved.

Keep practical details easy to find

Opening hours, address, parking, menus, booking options, dietary notes and contact routes should be obvious.

Support events and seasonal menus

PBI can help add pages or sections for specials, private hire, catering, tasting nights or seasonal offers.

Give search engines useful context

Good menu wording, local details and specific page titles help the site explain what kind of food business it is.

Can I add menus?

Yes. Menus can be added as page content or linked documents.

Can I show opening hours?

Yes. Add them to the contact page, footer or homepage sections.

Can customers book or enquire?

Yes. Buttons can go to a booking page, contact form or external booking system.
